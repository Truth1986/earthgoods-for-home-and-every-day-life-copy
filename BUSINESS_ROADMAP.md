## 💰 Merchandise & Sales Operations

### **Step-by-Step Sales Process**

```
CUSTOMER JOURNEY → REVENUE GENERATION
```

#### **Step 1: Customer Discovery**
- Customer finds EarthGoods via:
  - Social media (Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest)
  - Search engines
  - Referral links
  - Sponsored ads
- Views product catalog

#### **Step 2: Product Selection**
- Customer browses products
- Applies filters (price, category, eco-score)
- Reads reviews
- Adds items to cart

#### **Step 3: Referral Code Application** (Optional)
- Customer enters referral code (if has one)
- Discount automatically applied
- Saves 10-30% typically

#### **Step 4: Checkout**
- Select shipping method (Standard or Overnight)
- Enter address
- Choose payment method
- System calculates:
  - Product subtotal
  - Shipping cost (or 20% overnight fee)
  - **Platform fee: 3% of subtotal + shipping**
  - Discount (if applicable)
  - **Total = Subtotal + Shipping + Fee - Discount**

#### **Step 5: Payment Processing**
- Stripe processes payment
- Order created with "pending" status
- Order marked as "paid"
- Inventory reduced

#### **Step 6: Supplier Notification**
- System groups items by supplier
- Sends order details to each supplier
- Supplier prepares shipment

#### **Step 7: Shipment & Tracking**
- Supplier ships order
- Tracking number entered
- Customer receives tracking email
- Order status: "shipped"

#### **Step 8: Delivery & Follow-up**
- Customer receives order
- Order marked: "delivered"
- Loyalty points awarded (1 point per $1)
- Customer can leave review
- Option to return within 30 days

#### **Step 9: Return/Exchange (Optional)**
- Customer initiates return
- Admin approves
- Prepaid shipping label sent
- Customer ships back
- Refund processed upon receipt
